Leivas Email Triage System

Complete rule set for the AI-powered email management system running on local Ollama (qwen3:32b)

🧠 Classification Pipeline LIVE

Every email goes through Ollama first. Headers are only used as a safety net after.

1
Ollama Classification (Every Email)
qwen3:32b reads the sender, subject, and snippet. Classifies into one of: TO_RESPOND, ACTION_NEEDED, MEETING, DEAL_OM, INVESTOR, COMMENT, NOTIFICATION, INVOICE, FYI, MARKETING. This is the primary decision-maker.
2
CC Check Override
If you're in the CC field (not To), category is set to FYI regardless of what Ollama said. CC'd emails always stay in inbox.
3
Unsubscribe Header Safety Net
If Ollama classified something as non-marketing BUT the email has a List-Unsubscribe header → override to MARKETING. Catches anything Ollama missed. Exception: CC'd emails keep their FYI status.
📥 Inbox Rule LIVE

Simple two-line rule. No VIP lists, no reputation tiers.

is_marketing = category == "MARKETING" or (has_unsubscribe and not is_cc)
archive = is_marketing

✅ Stays in Inbox

  • TO_RESPOND — someone expecting a reply
  • ACTION_NEEDED — sign, approve, confirm
  • MEETING — calendar invites, scheduling
  • INVESTOR — LP communications
  • FYI — emails you're CC'd on

📤 Archived (Labeled Only)

  • MARKETING — spam, promos, unsubscribe emails
  • COMMENT — thread replies, comments
  • NOTIFICATION — automated alerts
  • DEAL_OM — OMs and listings (browse in folder)
  • INVOICE — bills and receipts (browse in folder)
  • UNCLASSIFIED — anything AI isn't sure about

✏️ Auto-Draft Replies NEEDS RE-AUTH

When Drafts Are Created

  • Only for TO_RESPOND emails
  • Uses voice profile from 283 analyzed sent emails
  • Draft saved to Gmail Drafts in the original thread
  • Appears in Gmail ready to review and send

When Drafts Are NOT Created

  • ACTION_NEEDED (sign/approve — no written reply needed)
  • INVESTOR (needs careful manual reply)
  • MEETING (just accept/decline)
  • Anything classified as marketing

Hourly Digest

  • Batches all triage results into one message per hour
  • Posts to #ai-triage (private, just you)
  • Shows totals: emails processed, archived, drafts created
  • Sorted by priority within each account

Action Reports (Every 30 min)

  • Auto-unsubscribes (senders with 5+ marketing hits)
  • Smart forwarding alerts (wrong inbox detection)
  • Snooze reminders (unanswered 24h+ TO_RESPOND)
  • Contact enrichment (unknown business domain senders)
